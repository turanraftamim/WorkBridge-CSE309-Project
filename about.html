About WorkBridge

Empowering Bangladesh's workforce through technology and opportunity

Our Mission

WorkBridge is a decentralized web application designed to connect job seekers and service providers directly with employers in a simple, transparent, and secure way. We're bridging the gap between opportunity and talent in Bangladesh's growing economy.

Our platform helps open opportunities for more people, especially informal workers, and supports businesses by making hiring easier and more trustworthy.

The Problem

  • Workers and freelancers in Bangladesh struggle to find reliable job opportunities
  • Existing platforms mainly focus on white-collar jobs
  • Informal workers rely on word-of-mouth or social media, which is unorganized and unsafe
  • Limited access to verified opportunities and secure payment systems

Our Solution

  • Create detailed profiles showcasing skills and experience
  • Post jobs or services with transparent requirements
  • Search listings by location and specific skills
  • Communicate directly without intermediaries
  • Secure payment and verification systems
