About WorkBridge

Many workers and freelancers in Bangladesh struggle to find reliable job opportunities because existing platforms mainly focus on white-collar jobs. Informal workers often rely on word-of-mouth or social media, which is unorganized and unsafe.

WorkBridge is a decentralized web application designed to connect job seekers and service providers directly with employers in a simple, transparent, and secure way. Users can create detailed profiles, post jobs or services, search listings by location and skills, and communicate directly without intermediaries.

This platform helps open opportunities for more people, especially informal workers, and supports businesses by making hiring easier and more trustworthy. Challenges include verifying users and encouraging adoption in areas with limited internet access.

Despite challenges, WorkBridge aims to improve employment opportunities and support Bangladesh’s growing workforce by leveraging technology and community engagement.
