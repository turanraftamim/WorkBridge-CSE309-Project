Help / FAQ

How do I create a profile?

Click "Sign Up" on the login page and fill out your details including skills, location, and contact info.

How can I post a job or service?

Go to "Post Job/Service", fill out the required information, and submit it.

How do I apply for a job?

Browse jobs under "Find Jobs/Services", then click on a listing to apply.

Who do I contact for support?

Use the contact form on the left. We respond within 24–48 hours.
