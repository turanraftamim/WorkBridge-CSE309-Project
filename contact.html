Help Center & FAQ

How do I create a profile?

Click "Sign Up" on the login page and fill out your details including skills, location, and contact info. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.

How can I post a job or service?

Go to "Post Job/Service" from the main menu, fill out the required information including title, description, budget, and required skills. Once submitted, your posting will be reviewed and activated within 24 hours.

How do I apply for a job?

Browse jobs under "Find Jobs/Services", then click on a listing to view details. Click the "Apply Now" button to submit your application. You can include a cover letter and your portfolio if needed.

Who do I contact for support?

Use the contact form on this page or email us directly at support@workbridge.com. We respond to all inquiries within 24–48 hours. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. The link will expire after 24 hours for security reasons.
